Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between the Basic, Plus, and Premium plans?

The Basic plan is free and includes core features like joining and creating study groups. The Plus plan unlocks additional features, including discussion forums and study spaces, while the Premium plan offers all features, such as access to interest-based groups and resource sharing.

Can I cancel or switch my plan at any time?

Yes, you can cancel or switch plans anytime. If you decide to upgrade or downgrade, your changes will take effect immediately for a seamless experience.

Is there a free trial for the Plus or Premium plan?

StudyHub offers a free plan with limited features. If you’re interested in the Plus or Premium features, you may try them out with a 7-day free trial to explore advanced functionalities.

How does StudyHub protect my privacy and data?

StudyHub takes data privacy seriously. Your information is secure and will only be shared with group members if you choose to join or create a study group. We adhere to data protection regulations and implement the latest security measures.

Can I share resources and files with my study group?

Yes, resource sharing is available in the Premium plan. Members in the Premium tier can upload and share files and resources within their groups for efficient collaboration.
